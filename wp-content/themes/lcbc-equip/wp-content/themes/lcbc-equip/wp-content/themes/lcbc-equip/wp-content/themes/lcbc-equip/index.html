EQUIP 2023 // JUNE 2-4

Holy Sexuality & The Gospel

The EQUIP Conference is an annual church-wide event hosted at our church with the aim of equipping LCBC with biblical teaching on a specific, relevant topic. To more closely align with the intentions of the conference, what was previously known as the Church Family Conference (CFC), is now renamed to the EQUIP Conference.

As more confusion abounds on sexuality and gender, we have invited Dr. Christopher Yuan to articulate how God’s truth is crystal clear not gray. Through the narrative of his personal transformation from an agnostic gay man to a Bible professor and through his graduate level education in biblical languages, theology, and even health sciences, Dr. Yuan casts a compelling vision of not embracing our sexuality, but embracing Christ, thus shattering the paradigms of gay and straight and pointing us toward the biblical standard of holy sexuality.

About Dr. Christopher Yuan

Dr. Yuan graduated from Moody Bible Institute in 2005 and received a master’s in biblical exegesis in 2007 and a doctorate of ministry in 2014. Christopher taught the Bible at Moody Bible Institute for twelve years and his speaking ministry on faith and sexuality has reached five continents. He speaks at conferences, on college campuses, and in churches. 

He has co-authored with his mother their memoir, Out of a Far Country: A Gay Son’s Journey to God, A Broken Mother’s Search for Hope, which has sold over 100,000 copies and is now in eight languages. Dr. Yuan’s newest book, Holy Sexuality and the Gospel: Sex, Desire, and Relationships Shaped by God’s Grand Story, was named 2020 Book of the Year for Social Issues by Outreach Magazine.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is there a cost for attending? What does it cover?

There will be a small fee of $25 per adult for members of LCBC and $30 per adult for non-members. This will help cover costs for the following:

  • Saturday morning snacks
  • Saturday lunch
  • Resources on Biblical Sexuality and other topics, while supplies last
When do I pay my registration fee?

There are two ways you can pay: pay online when you register to attend, or pay at the door when you arrive in-person for EQUIP. If you choose to pay at the door, select “Pay Later” when you are registering online.

When is the last day to register?

We will keep registration open until the day of the EQUIP, but if we get enough registrations where we would max out the room capacity, then we would need to close registration at that point.

Is there childcare?

On-site childcare is available for kids 3rd grade and under. We will be providing snacks for childcare, but we ask parents to bring snacks for their children if they have any allergy concerns. 

Will there be a livestream to watch online?

We do not plan to live-stream the conference. We encourage everyone to come out in-person for the full-experience. 

Will this only be in English?

Mandarin translation will be available in the main room of the ETC (red building).

Can I invite people who do not attend LCBC?

EQUIP is intended primarily for our church family at LCBC. However, we hope we can bless others from other church families as well. To that end, the registration timeframe will be as follows:

  • Until May 5: Open registration for all LCBC members and attendees
  • May 5 – June 2: Open registration for anyone
Where should I go when I arrive?

Upon arrival, check-in at the registration table in the MPR (the gray and white building) where main events will be hosted. Childcare will be located in the ETC (the red building).

I have another question.

Have more questions? Email us at information@lagunabaptist.org.
